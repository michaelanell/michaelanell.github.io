Taget Audience

The target audience for this site is those who want to know the weather in the specified locations. The target age would be 12 to 90+ and the device most prople would most likely view the site on would be a mobile device.

Persona One

The first personna for this site is a 15 year old boy. He plays soccer and this is his first year on the varsity team. His first game is coming up, and wants to know if the expected weather will be warm, windy, or rainy as it will affect the game.

Perosna Two

The second persona is an 18 year old girl going to her senior prom. She wants to know the weather as it is sping and could be rainy. She wants to know if she should have an umbrella so that her hair and makeup doesn't get ruined.

Senarios

  1. I want to take my kids to the park, but I don't want them to get sick if it is too cold, so I will check the weather.
  2. I spent a lot of time on my hair today, and I know it will get frizzy if I go outside. I will check the weather to see if I should or not.
  3. My dance recital is this afternoon, and I have to have my hair in a certain way for it. If it is raining, I must take an umbrella, so I will check the weather.
  4. I am planning to garden today, and need to know if I need a strong SPF or not so that I do not get sunburned, so I will check the weather.
  5. I am inviting my friends to go on a picnic, but need to check the weather first so I can make sure it will be appropriate.

Taget Audience

The target audience for this site is those who want to know the weather in the specified locations. The target age would be 12 to 90+ and the device most prople would probably view the site on would be a mobile device.

Persona One

The first personna for this site is a 15 year old boy. He plays soccer and this is his first year on the varsity team. His first game is coming up, and wants to know if the expected weather will be warm, windy, or rainy as it will affect the game.

Perosna Two

The second persona is an 18 year old girl going to her senior prom. She wants to know the weather as it is sping and could be rainy. She wants to know if she should have an umbrella so that her hair and makeup doesn't get ruined.
